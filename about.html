What is Afterbetting?

A self-guided gambling recovery platform built by someone who spent 8 years inside the addiction and 2 years finding a way out.

The short answer

Afterbetting is a gambling recovery app that gives people who want to stop gambling a private daily structure to stay clean, rebuild their finances, and feel like themselves again. It is not a clinical service, a forum, or a streak tracker that stops there. It is a complete daily toolkit built from lived experience.

It is free to start. No credit card required. Available in English, Dutch, German, French, and Spanish.

Who it is for

Afterbetting is for adults who have recognised that gambling has become a problem and want to stop. Primarily sports bettors, casino gamblers, and online poker players. People who are in early recovery, in the middle of recovery, or who have relapsed and need to start again.

It is not a medical service and does not replace clinical treatment. It works alongside therapy, Gamblers Anonymous, and other professional support as a daily accountability and structure tool.

What it does

  • Recovery streak tracker. Tracks gambling-free days from a chosen start date. Milestone celebrations at 7, 14, 30, 60, 90, 180, and 365 days.
  • Daily guided journal. Over 100 prompts personalised by age, gender, and recovery stage. Write, save, and review entries privately.
  • Habit builder. Set daily, weekly, and monthly habits to replace gambling with structure. Tracks completion and builds streaks.
  • Financial recovery dashboard. Track debts, set repayment goals, monitor savings, and see what gambling losses would be worth invested. Available on Progress and Premium plans.
  • AI Anchor. A private, judgment-free AI recovery companion available at any time. Responds in the language you write in. Detects crisis language and shows local helpline numbers immediately. Available on Premium plan.
  • Support circle. A private inner circle for accountability with trusted people. Available on Premium plan.
  • Crisis button. Available on all plans. Shows breathing exercises, grounding messages, and local gambling helpline numbers for the user's country.
  • Weekly email report. Summary of streak, mood, and habit progress. Available on Progress and Premium plans.

How it compares to alternatives

Most gambling recovery tools do one thing: track a streak. Afterbetting combines streak tracking, journaling, habit building, financial recovery, AI companion support, and a support circle in one private platform. No competitor in the European market combines all of these features at this depth.

Gambless is the closest competitor with a mobile app and basic journaling. QuitGamble focuses on content rather than product. I Am Sober and RecoverMe are generic addiction apps not built specifically for gambling recovery. None have a financial recovery dashboard or an AI companion.

Pricing

The free plan is a genuine recovery tool, not a teaser. It includes the streak tracker, three journal prompts per week, three habit slots, a basic dashboard, and the crisis button with no time limit.

The Progress plan at 9.99 per month unlocks unlimited journaling, unlimited habits, the full financial recovery dashboard, mood analytics, and weekly email reports.

The Premium plan at 19.99 per month adds the AI Anchor companion, the support circle, the debt planner, financial goals and milestone tracking, and priority email support.

An annual plan is available at 79.99 per year, equivalent to 6.67 per month.

Who built it

Afterbetting was built by a founder who spent more than 8 years as a sports bettor before stopping completely. The founder is now more than 2 years gambling-free and completely debt-free. The product was built because nothing like it existed during recovery, and every feature reflects something that was missing when it was needed most.

The company is based in the Netherlands. The product is available worldwide.

Is it safe and private?

All journal entries are private. No one else reads them. The platform does not share personal data with third parties for advertising purposes. The AI Anchor conversation history is stored securely and is only accessible to the logged-in user. Users can delete their conversation history at any time.

Afterbetting is not a medical service. If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, please contact a licensed professional or a gambling helpline in your country.
