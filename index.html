FAQ

Common questions about quitting gambling

Is Afterbetting free to use?

Yes. The free plan includes your recovery streak, 3 journal prompts per week, 3 habit slots, and a basic dashboard. No credit card required, no time limit.

How does Afterbetting help me stop gambling?

Afterbetting gives you daily structure through a recovery streak, guided journal prompts, habit tracking, and financial tools. It is a self-guided platform you use at your own pace — available 24/7.

Is this a medical service or therapy?

No. Afterbetting is not a medical service or a substitute for professional treatment. If you are in crisis, please contact a licensed professional or a gambling helpline in your country.

Which countries is Afterbetting available in?

Afterbetting is available worldwide.

What makes Afterbetting different from other apps?

Most apps track your streak and stop there. Afterbetting combines daily journaling, habit building, financial recovery tracking, a support circle, and an AI companion in one warm, private platform.

Can I use Afterbetting alongside professional help?

Absolutely. Afterbetting works well alongside therapy, Gamblers Anonymous, or any other support. Many users combine it with professional help as their daily accountability tool.
